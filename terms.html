Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using the HSRP Online Portal (transportinfo.online), you acknowledge and agree to comply with these Terms and Conditions. This portal is operated under the guidelines of the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH), Government of India, for facilitating High Security Registration Plate (HSRP) services.

2. Scope of Services

This portal provides online application services for:

  • New HSRP Number Plates for Two Wheelers and Four Wheelers
  • Replacement of damaged or lost HSRP plates
  • Retention of existing registration numbers during vehicle transfer
  • Ownership transfer-related HSRP re-issuance
3. User Obligations

Users must provide accurate and complete information as per their vehicle Registration Certificate (RC). Any false, misleading, or fraudulent information submitted through this portal may result in rejection of the application and may attract legal action under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 and IT Act, 2000.

4. Data Verification

All information submitted through this portal is verified against the National Vahan Database maintained by the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. Discrepancies in vehicle registration number, chassis number, or owner details may lead to application rejection.

5. Payment & Fees

HSRP fees are determined as per the rates notified by the Central Government and respective State/UT RTOs. The portal may collect applicable service charges. All payments are processed through secure government-approved payment gateways. Refund policies are subject to the rules of the respective RTO.
