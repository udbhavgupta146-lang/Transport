About HSRP

High Security Registration Plate — Mandatory for all vehicles in India

Tamper-Proof Security

HSRP plates use chromium-based hologram and laser-branding that cannot be duplicated.

  • Hot-stamped chromium hologram
  • Laser-branded unique number
  • IND inscription with Ashoka Chakra
Supreme Court Mandate

As per Supreme Court directive, HSRP is mandatory for all old and new vehicles across India.

  • Applicable to two & four wheelers
  • Commercial vehicles included
  • Penalty for non-compliance
Verified & Trackable

Each plate is linked to the vehicle chassis and engine number in the national database (Vahan).

  • Snap-lock non-removable fitting
  • National database integration
  • Easy verification by authorities
